Epithelial Mechanics Fan Club

Together with Nimesh Chahare, I run the Epithelial Mechanics Fan Club, a vibrant online community. We provide young researchers from all over the world a platform to share their research interests in mini-review threads and post articles on topics related to #EpithelialMechanics in various contexts, from in vitro studies to embryology.
